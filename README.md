# Ghost Caddie

AI-powered golf swing analysis in your pocket.

## What Is Ghost Caddie?

Ghost Caddie is a mobile app that uses AI and your smartphone camera to analyze your golf swing and provide instant, professional-grade feedback — without the $200/hour coaching fees or $15,000 launch monitors.

## Features

- 📱 **Record with Your Phone** — No special equipment needed
- 🤖 **AI-Powered Analysis** — 18+ key body points analyzed
- ⚡ **Instant Feedback** — Get results in seconds
- 🎯 **Personalized Drills** — AI-generated practice recommendations
- 📈 **Progress Tracking** — See your improvement over time
- 🏌️ **Compare to Pros** — Overlay your swing against tour players
